GEARSHARE BORROWER AGREEMENT

By accepting this agreement, you agree to the following terms:

  1. Return Condition: You agree to return the item in the same condition as when borrowed, accounting for normal wear and tear.
  2. Timely Return: Items must be returned by the due date. Late returns will result in:
    • 10 credit points deducted per day for the first 3 days
    • 20 credit points deducted per day after 3 days
    • Automatic reporting to system administrators after 7 days
  3. Damage/Loss: You are responsible for any damage or loss occurring while the item is in your possession.
  4. Inspection: All items will be inspected upon return. You may be asked to provide photos if returning by mail.
  5. Account Suspension: Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent suspension of borrowing privileges.
